About Us

We are a community of avid travellers, who found happiness in adventure! Adventure should not be restricted to just an activity. It should be treated like sports, taking in account the extreme adrenaline rush, the physical drive and the required skill set.
We wanted to make this beautiful feeling accessible for every individual. This was the embryonic purpose of this platform. And today we offer you over 10 adventure sports in the beautiful land of India. With lofty snow-clad peaks, gushing rivers, steep gorges, picturesque valleys, wide seas and sweeping desert plains, India truly has destinations perfect for extreme adventure and an unexplored potential in Sports.
We created this platform and want to grow it with an aim to: impart information about adventure sports in India and to train, guide and make one experience extreme adventure the right way by on boarding the best of schools and operators with a proven safety record.
Dive in the zestful world of adventures! We are right here waiting for you!!!
